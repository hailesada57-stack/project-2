About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a cool anime about curses and sorcerers. The main guy is Yuji Itadori who eats a finger and becomes strong.

It's based on manga by Gege Akutami. Lots of action and fights!

Why I like it

The animation is awesome and characters are fun.
